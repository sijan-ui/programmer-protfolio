UHSC

Uttara High School and College is a school and college located in Uttara, Dhaka. It is located a few meters away from the Dhaka-Mymensingh highway and about two kilometers from Shahjalal International Airport in Sector 6 of Uttara. The institute was established in 1985 in Uttara. Founding period 1985 (School Branch), 2009 (College Branch).

DIU

Daffodil International University (DIU) is a private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was founded in 2002.The original campus is located at Dhanmondi and the permanent campus is located in Daffodil Road, Khagan, Ashulia, Savar which is in Dhaka district.
